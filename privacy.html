AICord Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024.04.14

Thank you for choosing AICord! This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by AICord ("we" or "us") in connection with your use of our Discord bot and its associated dashboard (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Server Data: When you invite AICord to your Discord server, we collect the unique identifier of your server (server ID) to provide you with the Services.
  • User Data: We may collect certain user data to facilitate the functioning of AICord, such as premium status, tokens, and usage limits.
  • Character Data: AICord may store AI character data, such as character profiles, to enhance your experience within the Services.
  • Referral Information: If you refer other users to AICord, we may collect information about those referrals for administrative purposes.
  • Alert Information: We may collect information related to alerts generated by AICord, including whether they have been read or not.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing and Improving the Services: We use your information to provide, maintain, and improve AICord and its features, as well as to develop new features and functionalities.
  • Communications: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about your use of the Services, including responding to your inquiries and providing customer support.
  • Analytics: We may use your information for analytics purposes, such as to understand how users interact with AICord and to improve our services.
  • Compliance: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes, or to enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties when you give us consent to do so.
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if we believe it is necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of AICord, its users, or others.

4. Data Retention

We retain the information we collect for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by posting the updated policy on our website or through other appropriate means.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices regarding your information, please contact us at our Discord support server.

By using AICord, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
